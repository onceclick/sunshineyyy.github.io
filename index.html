Short Bio

Yaoyu Yang is a Ph.D. student in Electrical Engineering. He received his B.S. in Automation from Shanghai Jiao Tong University in 2011. He began his graduate study in UW EE in fall 2011 and found himself fascinated by the emerging area of synthetic biology. He joined Klavins lab since then and has been working on several projects in synthetic biology with the aim of designing, building and characterizing engineered biological system to process information and perform computation. He works on both theoretical and experimental sides.
